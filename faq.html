Assembly Label is a contemporary Australian fashion brand founded in 2011, specializing in minimalist designs and quality basics. The brand focuses on creating timeless wardrobe essentials with clean lines and modern silhouettes that transcend seasonal trends. Their collections emphasize natural fibers like organic cotton, linen, and merino wool, with particular attention to fabric quality and garment construction. Assembly Label pieces are designed to work together across seasons, allowing customers to build cohesive wardrobes rather than chasing trends. The brand has gained recognition for balancing aesthetic appeal with practical functionality, offering elevated basics that maintain their appearance through years of wear.

Assembly Label maintains high quality standards through careful fabric selection and construction methods. How should I care for Assembly Label knitwear and jeans?

Assembly Label knitwear requires specific care to maintain quality. Merino wool jumpers should be hand-washed in cool water (below 85°F) using wool-specific detergent, or machine-washed on delicate cycle inside a mesh bag. Never use fabric softener, which coats fibers and reduces breathability. Lay flat to dry away from direct heat or sunlight, as hanging causes stretching and heat exposure causes shrinkage. Between wears, air garments for 24 hours rather than washing after each use—merino's natural antimicrobial properties resist odors. Store folded rather than hung to prevent shoulder dimples. For jeans, wash inside-out in cold water every 5-10 wears to preserve color and fabric integrity. Avoid hot water and high-heat drying, which cause shrinkage and accelerate wear. Line drying maintains shape and extends lifespan significantly. Spot-clean minor stains rather than full washing. The organic cotton denim will soften and mold to your body over time, developing natural fading patterns that enhance rather than detract from appearance.
